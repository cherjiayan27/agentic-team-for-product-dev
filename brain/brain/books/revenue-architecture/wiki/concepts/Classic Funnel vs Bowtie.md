# Classic Funnel vs Bowtie

## Definition

The shift from the 100-year-old marketing-and-sales funnel to the [[Bowtie]] as the canonical system for recurring revenue. Funnel captures acquisition only (the original purpose, designed for ownership/perpetual sales). Bowtie extends to cover the full customer journey including onboarding, retention, and expansion — where recurring revenue actually happens.

## Comparison

| Dimension | Classic Funnel | Bowtie |
|---|---|---|
| Scope | Acquisition only | Entire customer journey |
| Monetization era | Ownership / perpetual | Subscription / consumption |
| Direction | One-directional (top-down) | Bidirectional with feedback |
| Growth source | Acquisition | Acquisition + Retention + Expansion |
| Measurement | Leads, deals closed, discount, sales cycle, NPS | ARR, growth rate, CAC Payback, CTS, FCF, MAU, DAU, GRR, NRR per GTM motion |
| Decision basis | Budget + ROI | Priority (via [[Critical Event]]) |
| Right customer | Those that don't churn | Those that expand |
| Feedback | Limited; delayed (mostly NPS) | Continuous closed-loop |
| Rallying cry | "Always Be Closing" | "No recurring impact. No recurring revenue." |

## The funnel's five constraints

1. **Doesn't cover recurring revenue** — 2/3 of growth happens outside it
2. **Seller-centric** — deal-closing mindset; ignores Impact delivery
3. **One-directional** — assumes linear progression; real customers zigzag
4. **Treated linearly by operators** — "double leads for double revenue" ignores [[Nonlinearity]]
5. **No closed loops** — no structural feedback

## The Bowtie's answer

Eight sub-systems (Figure 4.3):
1. Awareness
2. Education
3. Selection
4. Mutual Commit
5. Onboarding
6. Adoption (Retention)
7. Expansion
8. The GTM System (all 7 together)

Plus the [[Data Structure]] overlay (VM / CR / Δt / Performance) added in Ch 6.

## What to preserve from the funnel

Not everything about the funnel is obsolete. Ch 4 emphasises preserving:
- **A century of accumulated knowledge** about awareness, education, and selection
- **Proven acquisition patterns** that translate (ABM, MEDDIC, etc.)
- **Role clarity for acquisition-focused functions**

The Bowtie doesn't replace the funnel — it *extends* it into the stages where recurring revenue is earned.

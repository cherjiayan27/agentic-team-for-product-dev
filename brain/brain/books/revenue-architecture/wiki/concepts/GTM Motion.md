# GTM Motion

## Definition

A structured set of activities, tactics, and processes spanning the entire customer journey — from finding prospects to converting them to satisfying them as long-term customers. The **production-line equivalent** in the [[Recurring Revenue Factory]]. Involves cross-functional collaboration across marketing, sales, and customer success.

## The production-line analogy

Figure 1.5: a recurring revenue business runs multiple GTM motions simultaneously, each producing its own ARR stream:

- **GTM Motion A** → ARR(A) with metrics/costs specific to motion A
- **GTM Motion B** → ARR(B) ...
- **GTM Motion C** → ARR(C) ...

Total ARR = Σ ARR across motions. Each motion has its own [[Growth Formula]].

## Anatomy of a motion

A GTM motion involves:

- **Dozens of micro-actions**
- **Multiple departments** (at least marketing + sales + CS)
- **Specific tools and [[Means and Methods]]**
- **A defined customer journey** (the [[Bowtie]])
- **Its own Growth Formula and [[Unit Economics]]**

## Types of GTM motions

Captured across two frameworks:

- [[5 Touch GTM Motions]] — organised by engagement level: No / Low / Medium / High / Dedicated Touch
- [[GTM Model]] X-Y framework — organised by ACV × deal volume

Specific motions:
- [[No Touch GTM Motion]] (product-led / self-serve; [[Product-Led Growth]])
- [[Medium Touch GTM Motion]] (SDR + AE inbound/outbound)
- [[High Touch GTM Motion]] (field sales + ABM)
- [[Dedicated Touch GTM Motion]] (specialised teams per F500 account)

## Why motions matter

- **One motion = one production line.** Mixing motions without coordination = factory chaos.
- **Each motion matures separately.** [[GTM Fit]] must be achieved per motion.
- **[[12 Revenue Breakpoints]] apply per motion.**
- **Costs don't linearly combine.** Each extra motion **squares** the [[Means and Methods]] interconnections.

## Motion rules

- **≤3 motions until $50M ARR** (from [[GTM Model]] §10.2.1)
- **One [[Growth Formula]] per motion** — never average
- **Standardise the Interface ([[SPICED]])** to enable motion [[Interoperability]]

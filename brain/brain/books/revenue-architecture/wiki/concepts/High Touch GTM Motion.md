# High Touch GTM Motion

*Stub — full treatment in Ch 10. Partial definition from Ch 6's GTM-to-Bowtie mapping.*

## Definition

The enterprise-grade GTM motion in the [[GTM Model]]. Aimed at deals ranging from hundreds of thousands to millions of dollars. Uses specialist methods like Account-Based Marketing (ABM), multi-stakeholder selling, and multi-month sales cycles.

## Key characteristics (§6.4)

- **Deal Expansion is the real growth.** Initial deals are large but carry modest profit — the company invests heavily to fulfill the promise. Growth comes from expansion in later years.
- **Proof of Concept (POC).** Sales cycle can run a year+. Seller must prove integration with buyer's tool stack.
- **Targeting.** Because each account consumes significant resources, account selection is deliberate.
- **Referrals.** Lead gen relies heavily on existing customers advocating — often facilitated by a Customer Advisory Board (CAB).

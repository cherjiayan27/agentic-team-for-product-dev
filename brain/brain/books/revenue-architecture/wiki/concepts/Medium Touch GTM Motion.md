# Medium Touch GTM Motion

*Stub — full treatment in Ch 10. Partial definition from Ch 6.*

## Definition

The mid-market / SMB GTM motion. A lower-cost representative (SDR) handles outbound and inbound lead development, then hands off to a sales manager for the qualification and close. Higher velocity than [[High Touch GTM Motion]], lower volume than [[No Touch GTM Motion]].

## Key characteristics (§6.4)

- **Thought Leadership** — lead gen via high-value content (HubSpot's inbound-marketing expertise is canonical)
- **Email Sequencing** — automation-augmented outreach; sensitive to [[Law of Diminishing Returns]]
- **Churn Prevention** — high-velocity deals are the most sensitive to churn
- **Renewal-driven** — heavily reliant on high renewal rates; customers often remain 8–9 years

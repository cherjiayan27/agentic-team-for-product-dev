# CAC

*Stub — referenced in Ch 9, fully defined in Ch 10 ([[GTM Model]]).*

## Informal definition

**Customer Acquisition Cost.** Total sales and marketing cost required to acquire one new customer.

```
CAC = (Sales + Marketing costs) / new customers acquired in period
```

## Ch 9 context

- Rises disproportionately during downturns (Table 9.6 → 9.7: CAC as % of ARR grew from 45% to 72% when conversion rates declined ~5% across the board)
- Cornerstone of [[Sustainability]] analysis

## CAC Payback

Time required to recover CAC from the acquired customer's revenue. A key sustainability metric, typically expected to be <24 months for healthy SaaS.

# Cost to Serve

*Stub — referenced in Ch 9, fully defined in Ch 10 ([[GTM Model]]).*

## Informal definition

**CTS.** The ongoing cost of supporting and delivering value to an existing customer across their lifetime. Complements [[CAC]]:
- CAC = cost to acquire
- CTS = cost to retain + deliver impact

## Why it matters

- Rises disproportionately during downturns alongside CAC
- Affects the net margin of retained revenue — even high [[Net Revenue Retention]] can be unprofitable if CTS rises faster
- Central to [[Sustainability]] analysis per GTM motion

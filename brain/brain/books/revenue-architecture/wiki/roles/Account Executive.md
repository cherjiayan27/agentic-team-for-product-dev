# Account Executive

*Stub — full treatment in Ch 8. Partial definition from Ch 6.*

## Scope

The sales closer role. Accepts qualified opportunities from an [[Sales Development Representative]] (or self-sources in High Touch motions), runs discovery → proposal → negotiation → mutual commit. Compensation typically tied to commits closed (wins).

## Typical placement

- Core role in [[Medium Touch GTM Motion]] and [[High Touch GTM Motion]]
- Less central to [[No Touch GTM Motion]]

## Mathematical sensitivities

- Win rate = product of per-meeting conversion rates — see [[Win Rate]] and [[Law of Diminishing Returns]]
- Short-term commit focus risks damaging long-term [[Net Revenue Retention|NRR]] if the AE oversells or closes poorly-qualified customers (Rob vs Jennifer performance comparison in §6.2.4)

# Sales Development Representative

*Stub — full treatment in Ch 8. Partial definition from Ch 6.*

## Scope

The front-end sales role focused on **lead qualification and discovery-call booking**. Uses email, outbound calls, and sequencing tools to develop leads from VM2 → VM3/VM4. Hands off qualified opportunities to an [[Account Executive]] for the close.

## Typical placement

- Central to the [[Medium Touch GTM Motion]]
- Often omitted in [[High Touch GTM Motion]] (field reps generate their own opportunities, supplemented by ABM)
- Absent in pure [[No Touch GTM Motion]] (users self-qualify through the product)

## Sensitivities

- Subject to [[Law of Diminishing Returns]] — more emails in a sequence do not produce more conversions beyond a threshold
- Often the first role over-automated; AI-driven outbound at scale has caused diminishing effectiveness industry-wide

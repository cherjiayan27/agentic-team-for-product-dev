# Epilogue — The Pioneer Spirit

**Source:** `raw/markdown/12-epilogue.md` (book page 478)

## Thesis

A closing reflection, not a new framework. Jacco invokes the historical spirit of exploration — skyscrapers, bridges, moonshots, oceanic dives — and argues that Revenue Architecture is the next frontier for SaaS. The book's findings are intended as fuel for a new generation of pioneers who treat technological change as a puzzle, not a threat.

## Key claims

- The industry has "forgotten" the spirit of pioneering ambition
- The canvas ahead is as boundless as the sky
- AI and Factory 4.0 aren't threats to fear but puzzles to solve
- The story isn't over; the best days are ahead

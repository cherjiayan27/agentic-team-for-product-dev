# Bibliography

**Source:** `raw/markdown/15-bibliography.md` (book pages 517–518)

External works cited throughout *Revenue Architecture*. Useful as a reading list for deeper exploration of any particular topic.

## Key references surfaced in the wiki

- **Scott Page** — *The Model Thinker*. Intellectual foundation for [[Model Thinking]]; the idea that multiple overlapping models increase accuracy.
- **Reid Hoffman + Chris Yeh** — *Blitzscaling: The Lightning-Fast Path to Building Massively Valuable Companies*. Origin of [[Blitzscaling]] as a strategy.
- **Elias St. Elmo Lewis** — *The New Thought in Advertising* (1898). Origin of AIDA, the classic marketing-and-sales funnel that the [[Bowtie]] replaces.
- **W. Edwards Deming** — quality-management philosophy; processes, data-driven decisions, continuous improvement. Foundation for the [[Recurring Revenue Factory]] model.
- **David Skok** — *Touch levels* framework (2010). Origin of the [[Touch Concept]].
- **Neeraj Agrawal (Battery Ventures)** — 2015 piece on [[T2D3 Growth Trend]].
- **Daniel J. Adams** — [[Critical Event Timeline]] methodology from Ariba-era enterprise sales.
- **Kurt Vonnegut** — *Man in the Hole* story arc used for SPICED-based sales narratives.
- **Dave Boyce** — definition of [[Product-Led Growth]] ("users experience the product before purchasing").
- **Tomasz Tunguz** — data source for Adobe valuation and ARR evolution (Figures 5.14–5.15).
- **David Spitz / BenchSights** — [[CMGR]] as a preferred growth-rate measure.

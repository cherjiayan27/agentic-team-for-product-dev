# Amplitude Product Benchmark Report 2025 — Index

Per-source catalog. See root `_index.md` for cross-source view.

## Source

Amplitude's September 2025 Product Benchmark Report. 30 pages, 8 chapters. Empirical product-usage data from Amplitude's customer base (2,600+ companies, 10,600+ digital products, 171B monthly users; Sept 2023 – Sept 2024 data window).

**PDF:** `raw/pdf/amplitude-product-benchmark-2025.pdf`

## Chapters (sources)

- [[01-how-top-products-grow]] — Overview; top 10% outperform across all metrics; week-1 activation → 3-month retention (69% overlap); Leaky Bucket confirmed
- [[02-acquisition]] — Monthly acquisition benchmarks; top 10% = 8.7% monthly, 174% annual; no acquisition→retention correlation
- [[03-activation]] — Day 1/7/14 activation rates; top 10% = 21% day 1, median = 5%; "7 on day 7" rule; strong activation→retention correlation
- [[04-engagement]] — Monthly active user growth; top 10% = 7.5% / 138% annual; engagement ≠ retention (more leaky bucket)
- [[05-retention]] — Month 1/2/3 retention by percentile; enterprise 7× gap; 96% median churn by month 3 (pilot chapter)
- [[06-industry-snapshot]] — Per-industry benchmarks for 6 verticals; travel & hospitality leads across the board
- [[07-customer-experiences]] — Closing (Amplitude product pitch)
- [[08-methodology]] — Data sources, methodology, limitations

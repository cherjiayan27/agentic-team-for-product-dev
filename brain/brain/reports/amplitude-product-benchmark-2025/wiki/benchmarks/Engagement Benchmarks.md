# Engagement Benchmarks

## Definition

Cross-industry [[Engagement Rate]] benchmarks (monthly active user growth) from Amplitude's 2025 Product Benchmark Report.

## Data (Figures 8, 11)

### All products

| Percentile | Monthly engagement growth |
|---|---|
| 50th (median) | <1% |
| 90th (top 10%) | **7.5%** |

Annualised: median ~12%, top 10% ~**138%**.

**Concentration:** top 10% capture **79% of all user engagement**.

### Enterprise

| Percentile | Monthly engagement growth |
|---|---|
| 50th | 0.6% |
| 90th | **6.5%** |

Enterprise has narrower spread than all-products — engagement growth is hard at scale, and even top enterprises rarely exceed 8%.

## Interpretation

- **Monthly engagement growth >5%** = top-decile territory
- **1–5%** = above-median performance
- **<1%** = median; engagement is stagnant
- **Negative** = active base is shrinking; urgent problem

## Critical caveat

High engagement growth does NOT predict high retention ([[Leaky Bucket Problem]], Figure 10). Always pair this benchmark with [[Retention Benchmarks (All Products)]]. Engagement growing + retention weak = leaky bucket. Engagement growing + retention strong = durable hypergrowth.

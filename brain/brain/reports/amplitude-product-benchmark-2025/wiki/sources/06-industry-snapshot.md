# Industry Snapshot (Amplitude Benchmark Ch 6)

**Source:** `raw/markdown/06-industry-snapshot.md` (Amplitude Product Benchmark Report 2025, PDF pp. 25–28)

## Thesis

The four core metrics (Acquisition, Activation, Engagement, Retention) vary **meaningfully by industry**. Travel and hospitality lead across the board — median 3-month retention of 10% is higher than any other industry's median, and **top 10% travel retention hits 25.6%** (highest of all industries). Financial services and ecommerce follow. B2B tech, healthcare, and media/entertainment show more modest gains.

Notable outliers:
- **Media & entertainment median acquisition is negative** (-0.2%) — industry headwinds
- **Travel & hospitality median retention (10%) is higher than any other industry's top 10%** in some metrics — dramatic post-COVID rebound

## Pro tips by industry

Each industry has tailored tactics (from the report):

- **B2B tech:** Adopt PLG tactics — surface value moments early, guide users via self-serve onboarding, use product usage signals to drive expansion
- **Ecommerce:** Obsess over checkout flow — minimise friction in final steps before purchase; A/B test drop-off points
- **Financial services:** Invest in frictionless onboarding — finserv users churn fast when initial friction triggers doubt; mobile especially
- **Healthcare:** Make digital experience human — map patient/member journeys, fix confusing flows or missing context
- **Media & entertainment:** Personalisation is the antidote to churn — behavioural data to serve content/recommendations
- **Travel & hospitality:** Remove booking friction — inspiration is fleeting; fewer steps, transparent pricing, personalised results
